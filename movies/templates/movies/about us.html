about us

We are a sole proprietor specialized in software, hardware, cyber services and movies.

We are focused at solving customer problems by applying the required solution, in terms of good and services, in order to create a problem free society.

Our aim is to beat the local market through provision of world class services. This is our bid to improve your customer satisfaction.

We are making it our commitment to continue to improve our services, quality of services and span over more services and goods so that you may also feel at home with us. We are also committing to our "as cheap as possible" policy so that our customers may not be charged unless its necessary.
