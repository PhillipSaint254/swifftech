Automatic sorting

This feature is automatically enabled for all users. If you don't have an account, we show you movies generally liked by other people. These are either latest movies or movies that are generally viewed by many people. Once you open up an account and sig in, we this site learns the patterns of your interaction and tries to provide you with movies specific to your liking. For instance, It might seem that you really like action movies. This sites will learn that and suggest you action movies that are liked by other people. Please note that we have already categorised our movies to seperate movies with series. We have also categorised them according to genre so that you pick the genre of your liking incase movies suggested to you are not of your liking.

Efficient search

We have included a search function inorder for you to interact with our movies more specifically. This feature is available for all users. You can search your movies from the homepage of this site. Once this site loads, locate the input area for search and type the name of the movie you wish to watch. The site first checks for exact matches so for better match we encourage you to enter exact name of the movie, if you don't know the exact name or spelling of the movie you can try googling it... In the case where an exact match is not found, the site will look for movies that have a similar name, genre or description as your search. Thus the results may not be so accurate and may need you to do more filtering by yourself. If you can't locate the movie you want, please leave a request for the movie to be downloaded and/or be included in the site. The above three covers all corners making our search function efficient.

Subscription

Part of the reasons why this site was created was to give you the option to split your money between entertainment and basic needs, thus giving you a method of saving your money. Please note that we try to maximize your entertainment as cheaply as possible and leave you to your plans with the rest of your money. If you are an intense movie fun, we suggest you give the pro max subscription a shot. If you watch less, say three or four movies a week, the standard package is okay for you. If you watch 1 or two movies per day, we recommend pro package. If you watch movies only once in a while, you are fine not subscribing. Inorder for you to subscribe, deposit a minimum of the cost of the account you want to purchase, (300 for standard, 450 for pro and 600 for pro max). Find instructions about how to subscribe below. Once you have the amount, go to the homepage of this site. Scroll to find the subscription section. CLick on the card with the subscription you want. Once the card flips, click on the blue button to select that transaction. Proceed to confirm that you want to pay the amount prompted inorder to buy the account. Once you confirm, this account type will be subscribed to you.

Making a deposit

This feature is only available for users registered and logged in. To deposit, click on your username at the navigation bar. This will take you to your account page where you can view and/or change information about yourself. Under account information, click on the blue button saying deposit coins. Enter the amount you are depositing. If you are paying via mpesa, copy paste the transaction code to the second input box. If you are paying via cash, indicate in the second box that it's a cash deposit and surender the equivalent amount of money to us. We will verify your transaction and then save it. If your transaction is verified, the equivalent amount of your deposit will automatically reflect on your account.
